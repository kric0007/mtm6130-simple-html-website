Our Partners

At Whisker & Tail, we know it takes a village to keep tails wagging and whiskers twitching. Our trusted partners - from local shelters and responsible suppliers to independent creators and animal experts - are the heart of what we do.

They help us ensure every product we offer is thoughtfully chosen, ethically sourced, and pet-approved. Together, we're not just building a store - we're building a community that puts pets first, one paw at a time.
